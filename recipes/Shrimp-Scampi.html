Shrimp Scampi

A tasty and delicious pasta

This simple and delicious pasta is a must try in everyone's kitchen. It has a buttery rich flavor with just a hint of spice that really brings the dish together. This recipe is brought to you by Binging with Babish, a food content creator on youtube.

Ingredients

  • 2tsp Kosher Salt
  • Box of linguine pasta
  • 3 tbsp unslated butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • crushed red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lb frozen shrimp, defrosted and peeled
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • Juice of 1 large lemon
  • 2 Tbsp chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot filled with water & 2 teaspoons of salt to a boil.
  2. In a large skillet, heat your butter and oliveo oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Once melted add crushed garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until fragrant.
  4. Add pasta to boiling water and cook until al dente.
  5. Add your wine to the skillet and cook until reduced by half.
  6. Add your shrimp and cook on each side for about 1 min. Do not allow to overcook.
  7. Incorporate lemon juice into pasta.
  8. Drain pasta, but reserve 1/4 cup of the pasta water.
  9. Combine your pasta in the skillet. If sauce looks seperated, add some of the pasta water.
  10. Season to taste and garnish with parsely.
