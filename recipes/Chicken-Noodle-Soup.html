Chicken Noodle Soup

A juicy Chicken Noodle Soup to warm the soul on cold days.

This Chicken Noodle Soup is a unique twist packed full of flavor. It cobmines a mix of hearty herbs and spices with juicy tender chicken. This recipe is brought to you by Jauja Cocina Mexicana, a Mexican food content creator on youtube.

Ingredients

  • 5 Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 2 celery sticks
  • One small white onion
  • One young turnip
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 Bay leaves
  • Tsp of Thyme
  • 1/2 an onion diced
  • 1/2 cup of diced celery
  • 1/2 cup of diced carrots
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • Egg white noodles

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with 4L of water.
  2. Add Chicken thighs and salt.
  3. Add your onion, garlics, celery, parsley, 2 bay leaves, and thyme.
  4. Bring the water to a boil and place a lid on top, reduce heat to medium and leave it for 20 mins.
  5. Remove Chicken from stock and allow to cool before shredding.
  6. In a new pan fry the diced ingredients along with your last bay leaf and minced garlic. Only fry for about 3 minutes.
  7. Drain the stock we had previously made into our new pot; bring to a boil.
  8. Add your squash, noodles, and salt to taste.
  9. After 10 minutes add your chicken.
  10. The dish is ready to serve and enjoy. Feel free to garnish with chopped parsley.
